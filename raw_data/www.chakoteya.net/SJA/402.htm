The Nightmare Man, part two

Original Airdate: Oct 12, 2010

[Attic]

NIGHTMARE: Sleep tight, Lukey-boy.

[Luke's nightmare]

(A black space.)
LUKE: Mum.
(Back in the attic, the Nightmare man vanishes in a puff of smoke as Sarah Jane turns the door handle and enters. She covers Luke with a blanket and picks up the camcorder.)

[Luke's room]

(The Nightmare man walks through the wall to choose between Clyde and Rani.)
NIGHTMARE: Eeney-meeney, miny-mo, catch a tiger by the toe. If he squeals let him go, eeney-meeney, miny, mo. What sweet dreams. Soon change that.

[Attic]

(On the camcorder playback, Clyde is sleeping.)
RANI [OC]: He's such a lightweight.
SARAH JANE: Oh, Clyde.
LUKE [on screen]: Our world has always been full of nightmares, creatures and aliens who want to destroy us.

[Rani's nightmare]

(The Nightmare Man puts his purple energy into Rani's head. She wakes in her own lounge, on the sofa.)
LOUISE [on TV]: You're watching the BBC News and I'm Louise Marlowe. Later, we'll be looking at how the continuing rise in house prices has increased the number of families choosing to live in rented accommodation. But first, questions were asked in Parliament today.
(Rani tries to turn it off with the remote control.)
LOUISE [on TV]: Oh, that's nice. No, no, no, go ahead. Switch me off. I'm talking to you, Rani.
(Rani crawls across the carpet on hands and knees.)
LOUISE [on TV]: Didn't your mother ever tell you not to sit so close to the screen?
RANI: How are you doing that?
(Louise Marlowe's arm reaches out through the television screen and grabs Rani.)
RANI: No! No!
(Rani disappears into the television.)

[Luke's room]

NIGHTMARE: But I need more. I need so much more. Hello. Stop snoring.
(Clyde finds himself in a deserted fast food restaurant, behind the counter.)

[Attic]

LUKE [on screen]: It's the Nightmare Man and I can't stop him. Mum, I'm sorry. That's why I tried so hard to stay awake. No. Please.
(As Luke falls asleep on the couch, the camcorder gets a good view of the Nightmare Man.)
SARAH JANE: No. Luke! Come on, Luke, wake up. It's Mum, please. Come on, please. Wake up, please. Mister Smith, I need you. Come on, please. Wake up. K9! K9, where are you?
K9: Approaching, mistress. My sensors indicate Master Luke is asleep, mistress.
SARAH JANE: I know that, K9.
MR SMITH: I'm scanning the alien's face, Luke's Nightmare Man is a Vishclar from the Saretti dimension.
SARAH JANE: That creature stopped Luke telling us about him. Nut Luke's clever. He didn't record that video because he was scared but because he knew we'd find it, and he knew we'd save him.

[Clyde's nightmare]

(A old woman is there on a mobility scooter.)
SARAH JANE: You still serving burgers? I always knew you were a stupid boy.
CLYDE: Sarah Jane?

[Attic]

SARAH JANE: We've got to find a way to talk to him. Remember that sentient concrete disguised as the Chiswick flyover? She was using this to control people. If we can
K9: What is it, Mistress?
SARAH JANE: Clyde and Rani. Oh!

[Luke's room]

SARAH [OC]: Clyde? Rani?
(The Nightmare Man tiptoes away as Sarah Jane enters.)
SARAH JANE: Rani? Clyde? Clyde, come on, please. Clyde! Rani!

[Rani's nightmare]

(A television studio.)
RANI: What are you?
LOUISE: Breaking news, Rani. I need you.
RANI: What do you mean?
(Suddenly Rani is wearing a smart suit.)
RANI: I shouldn't be here. I don't know what I'm doing. You are a journalist, aren't you?

[Clyde's nightmare]

SARAH JANE: Go easy on the sauce.
CLYDE: I don't get it. Where's Luke?
SARAH JANE: Oh, he's such a clever boy. He's working for the Government now. Says he's going to be an astronaut. I went to space once.
CLYDE: Yeah, with the Doctor.
SARAH JANE: Oh, I told you? I get confused. The social reckon I'm losing it. They want to put me in a home. I said I've got a home, thank you very much.

[Rani's nightmare]

RANI: What am I going to say?
(Louise points to the teleprompter.)
RANI: Her name is Sarah Jane?
LOUISE: It's you and me, Rani. We're going to tell everyone who she is. We're going to destroy Sarah Jane Smith.

[Attic]

SARAH JANE: I can't wake them up.
MR SMITH: The sentient concrete. We must wake Luke first.
K9: Correct, Mister Smith.
SARAH JANE: Could you use this, K9? To talk to Luke, I mean.
K9: Affirmative. Working.
(K9 connects to the piece of concrete with wires around it.)
K9: Master Luke, can you hear me? Master Luke? I regret I have insufficient power.
MR SMITH: Perhaps I can help. If I were to connect to K9, I could boost his energy levels.
SARAH JANE: Oh, what do you need, Mister Smith? I've stuff here from planets across the universe. Mind control devices, alien plants. You just tell me what you need.
MR SMITH: I need a USB lead, Sarah Jane.

[Luke's nightmare]

(The laughter of the Nightmare Man fills what is now a corridor, with mathematical equations scrawled on the walls and red doors at intervals.)
LUKE: It isn't real. This isn't real. This is just a stupid dream.

[Outside Sarah Jane's home]

(The Nightmare Man runs down the stairs and outside into the night.)
LUKE [OC]: Nightmare Man. Nightmare Man. Can you hear me?
NIGHTMARE: Oh, your mind is so strong, Luke, echoing across the dimensions.

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: Come back and face me.

[Outside Sarah Jane's home]

NIGHTMARE: Hmm, no.

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: What's the matter? Are you scared?

[Outside Sarah Jane's home]

NIGHTMARE: I'm just happy. You've taken my place, I've taken yours. You've made the monster real, Lukey.
LUKE [OC]: Come back!
(The Nightmare Man goes into Bannerman Road.

[Attic]

SARAH JANE: Ready?
MR SMITH: Maximum energy to K9.
K9: Engaging.
MR SMITH: Approaching full energy transfer mode.

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: I can't stop him. I can't. It's just me.
K9: Negative, Master Luke. It is never just you.
LUKE: How did you get here? Did Mum send you?
K9: Affirmative. She wanted me to tell you that this is a dream, and that you need to wake up.
LUKE: I know, K9. I've tried, but I can't.

[Attic]

SARAH JANE: Mister Smith?
MR SMITH: The dog is communicating with Luke.

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: I wish Clyde and Rani were here, K9. They always know what to do.
K9: Master Clyde and Mistress Rani are asleep. Your mother cannot wake them.
LUKE: So he's got them.

[Rani's nightmare]

MAN [OC]: On air in sixty seconds.
RANI: Why do you want to destroy Sarah Jane?
LOUISE: Why do you think? The woman's a menace to society.
RANI: What are you on about? She saves the world.
LOUISE: At what cost, Rani? You, Clyde, her own son. How many times has she put you at risk?
RANI: It's not like that.
LOUISE: Answer the question. How many times have you nearly died because of Sarah Jane Smith?
RANI: I don't know.
LOUISE: You're just a child, Rani. It's not your fault. But if we don't expose her, she's going to get you killed.
RANI: She's given me a better life. She's my friend.
LOUISE: Oh, grow up. If you're going to be a journalist you've got to stop worrying about peoples' feelings. Don't you want to be a journalist?
RANI: Yes.
LOUISE: Then there's nothing wrong with us telling the world. The public have a right to know, Rani. And it's your responsibility as a journalist to tell them.
MAN [OC]: And on air in five, four

[Clyde's nightmare]

CLYDE: I don't want this. I was doing my art. I was good at it, wasn't I?
SARAH JANE: Oh, you are sweet. Stupid, but sweet.
CLYDE: I'm not stupid.
SARAH JANE: You were never as good as my Luke. That's why you ended up here.
CLYDE: You'd never say that.
SARAH JANE: What do you mean, dear?
CLYDE: This isn't a burger bar. These aren't real burgers, and you, you're not the real Sarah Jane.
(He tries to open the door.)
SARAH JANE: Oh, Clyde. There's no way out.

[Bannerman Road]

(The Nightmare Man sends his streams of energy to all the houses, and quivers in ecstasy.)
NIGHTMARE: The nightmares. So many nightmares for me to feast on.

[Rani's nightmare]

LOUISE: I'm Louise Marlowe.
RANI: And I'm Rani Chandra. Please don't make me do this.
LOUISE: A woman, living on a typical suburban street in London. Her name. Her name, Rani. What's her name.
RANI: Sarah Jane Smith.

[Luke's nightmare]

NIGHTMARE [OC]: Nightmares, so many nightmares.
LUKE: I'm having the nightmare and I'm here. Maybe they are as well. Clyde? Can you hear me? Rani?

[Rani's nightmare]

LUKE [OC]: Rani?
RANI: Luke, is that you?

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: It's not real. It's just a nightmare.

[Rani's nightmare]

LOUISE: Stay with me, Rani.
RANI: I can't wake up. I can't stop it.
LOUISE: Just read the news.
RANI: Luke, how do I get out of here?

[Luke's nightmare]

K9: She needs a door, Master Luke.
LUKE: That's it. Clyde? Clyde, can you hear me?

[Clyde's nightmare]

LUKE [OC]: Clyde?
CLYDE: Luke? Oh, thank God. Where are you?
LUKE [OC]: I'm going to get you out of there. Hold on.

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: Both of you listen. I'm in a corridor, and there's loads of doors. I need for you to concentrate. Imagine there's a door

[Rani's nightmare]

LUKE [OC]: And I'm on the other side of it.
RANI: Where are you?

[Clyde's nightmare]

CLYDE: Rani?

[Rani's nightmare]

CLYDE [OC]: We're in different nightmares, but we're talking to each other?

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: Think of a classroom door. Like the ones at school. You've got to imagine it. Do you understand?

[Clyde's nightmare]

LUKE [OC]: A red door. Imagine a red door.

[Rani's nightmare]

LUKE [OC] You can do it, I know you can. Focus. Keep focusing on the door.

[Clyde's nightmare]

LUKE [OC]: Focus!
CLYDE: It's working.

[Rani's nightmare]

RANI: I've done it!
LOUISE: Breaking news, Rani. Luke's leaving you. He's got his A-levels and he's going to university. He's leaving you behind.
RANI: I know.
LOUISE: Then why do you do what he tells you to? Stay with me, Rani, be a journalist. I'll give you everything you ever wanted.

[Clyde's nightmare]

SARAH JANE: Stay here. You made a door appear. You could make money, gold, everything you've ever wanted.
LUKE [OC]: Clyde?

[Rani's nightmare]

LUKE [OC]: Rani?

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: Please? I need you.

[Rani's nightmare]

RANI: Breaking news. I'm out of here.

[Luke's nightmare]

(Rani runs through the red door into Luke's corridor.)
LUKE: Come on. Come on!

[Clyde's nightmare]

CLYDE: I quit.

[Luke's nightmare]

LUKE: We did it.

[Bannerman Road]

(The energy tendrils suddenly stop.)
NIGHTMARE: What? No. No, that isn't possible. They're together!

[Luke's nightmare]

CLYDE: Are we, are we all having the same dream?
RANI: That's impossible.
LUKE: That's amazing.
CLYDE: I guess we're just that good. But where are we?
LUKE: Mister Smith told K9 about this different dimension. I think it's where our minds go when we dream.
K9: Reminder, we need to defeat the Nightmare Man.
RANI: The what now?

[Attic]

NIGHTMARE: Bad dog!
(The Nightmare Man zaps K9, sending him backwards across the floor.)
SARAH JANE: K9!
(Mister Smith gets the same treatment.)
NIGHTMARE: No help for you, Sarah Jane Smith. You must be alone, with a little hint of moonlight on your face.

[Luke's nightmare]

CLYDE: Here, boy.
LUKE: Something must have happened to the real K9. Mum!
RANI: Hey, she'll be OK. She's brilliant, your mum.
LUKE: You haven't seen him, Rani. You don't know what he's like.

[Attic]

NIGHTMARE: Good to meet you, at last.
SARAH JANE: You know me?
NIGHTMARE: I feed on every nightmare that every man, woman and child has ever had. And you have had so many. Oh, the things you have seen. Luke, your fear about what might happen to him. And the dreams about the Doctor, when he never came back and you thought he was dead. Oh, they were good.
SARAH JANE: I'm not scared of you. What's wrong? Not used to someone arguing back? What's the matter? If you're so strong, send me to sleep.
NIGHTMARE: You want to have nightmares?
SARAH JANE: You've already taken my son, my friends. This is already a nightmare. What else have I got live for? Come on, do it.
NIGHTMARE: You think if I send you to sleep, you'll be able to join Luke. Oh, and I almost fell for it.

[Luke's nightmare]

RANI: So, what do we do?
LUKE: I don't know.
RANI: It's okay, we're here.

[Attic]

NIGHTMARE: Why am I wasting my time with you, old woman? They're still together. I can feel them.
SARAH JANE: They're still together, even in their dreams. And that's what's hurting you.
NIGHTMARE: To be scared, he needs to be alone. And he will be alone. Forever.

SARAH JANE: Oh, no, no, no. No, come back! Help us!

[Luke's nightmare]

CLYDE: What the hell are you?

RANI: You're just an alien. We can stop you.
NIGHTMARE: You're nothing. You're pathetic children, scared of the dark. Scared of me.
RANI: Luke? Do something.
NIGHTMARE: You're going back into your nightmares.
CLYDE: Get away from us.
(Two doors open.)
NIGHTMARE: And you're going to be locked in them forever. Trapped for all eternity.
LOUISE [OC]: Breaking news, Rani. You're coming back to me.
SARAH [OC]: Oh, Clyde. You're so stupid.
NIGHTMARE: Night, night, children.
LUKE: No!
NIGHTMARE: Oh, your nightmares haven't even begun.
LUKE: Just listen to me.
NIGHTMARE: Why?
LUKE: Because you keep telling me about you and what you want. You've never asked about me.
NIGHTMARE: What are you talking about?
LUKE: But you're just an alien. Well, I'm called Luke Smith. I was made by aliens to destroy the world. But this brilliant woman, Sarah Jane Smith, she found me. And she made me good. And she found others. A boy called Clyde, and a girl called Rani. This world has faced so many nightmares but there's always been someone there to stop them. Us. You know, I was so scared because I thought my mates wouldn't be there for me. Never mind Oxford, they followed me into my nightmares. Three kids breaking down the walls of dreams to be together. Something no one's ever done. Because together, we're unbeatable.
NIGHTMARE: Oh, very good. But do you really think words can stop a creature like me?
LUKE: I wasn't talking to you.
RANI: He's right.
CLYDE: Because together, we stop the monsters.
(Luke, Clyde and Rani join hands.)
RANI: We stop the nightmares.
LUKE: Because when we're together, we're not scared. You've got no hold over us now.
CLYDE: Because we're brilliant when we're together.
NIGHTMARE: Get away from me.
LUKE: So, there we go. The boy made by aliens to destroy the world, saves the world yet again.
(The Nightmare Man is sucked through one of the doors.)

[Clyde's nightmare]

SARAH JANE: Oh, hello, love.
NIGHTMARE: No, I can't be trapped here. This can't happen to me. Let me out! Children of Earth, I demand you sleep and you dream, and you fear. You will fear me!
SARAH JANE: Come on, come and sit down. And I'll tell you all about my brilliant son.
NIGHTMARE: No.
SARAH JANE: His name's Luke Smith. And he's going to live happily ever after.
NIGHTMARE: Nooooooooooo!
